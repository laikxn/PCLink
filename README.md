## What is PCLink?

PCLink is a mobile app that lets you remotely control your Windows PC from your iPhone or Android device — from anywhere, not just your home network. Wake your PC, shut it down, lock it, browse files, control volume, sync your clipboard, take screenshots, and more — all from your phone.

## This Repository

This repo contains the **PCLink Agent** — the Windows background service that runs on your PC and communicates with the PCLink mobile app.

## Download

👉 **[Download the latest PCLink Agent for Windows](https://github.com/laikxn/PCLink/releases/latest)**

## Requirements

- Windows 10 or later
- PCLink app on iPhone, iPad, or Android

## Installation

1. Download `PCLink Agent.exe` from the [latest release](https://github.com/laikxn/PCLink/releases/latest)
2. Run it — if Windows shows a SmartScreen warning click **More info** → **Run anyway**
3. The agent will appear in your system tray
4. Open the PCLink app on your phone and follow the pairing instructions

## Features

- 🖥 **Remote Control** — Wake, shutdown, restart, and lock your PC
- 📁 **File Browser** — Browse, search, and download files from your PC
- 🎵 **Media Controls** — Play, pause, and skip tracks remotely
- 📋 **Clipboard Sync** — Share clipboard between your phone and PC
- 📷 **Screenshot** — Capture and save your screen from your phone
- 🔊 **Volume Mixer** — Control your PC's volume and individual app volumes
- 🎛 **Soundboard** — Play sounds through your PC speakers remotely
- ⬆️ **Upload Files** — Send files from your phone directly to your PC
- ⚡ **Custom Actions** — Launch any app or script from your phone
- 📅 **Scheduled Events** — Automate actions at set times
- 🎬 **Scenes** — Run multiple actions with one tap
- 📊 **System Stats** — Monitor CPU, RAM, and GPU usage in real time
- 🌐 **Works Anywhere** — Control your PC from any network, not just home Wi-Fi
- 🔒 **Secure** — Token-based authentication. No data ever collected or stored.

## Privacy

PCLink does not collect any data. Everything stays between your phone and your PC. See our [Privacy Policy](https://pclink.app/privacy).
